MY STORY

From Bushenyi roots
to global impact

Nakanjako Nuswaibah discovered her passion for learning at Pearl Junior School in Bushenyi, where Music, Dance & Drama and sports ignited her leadership spirit. As Dormitory Captain and later Head Prefect at Prime Junior School, she built integrity early — graduating with an impressive PLE Aggregate 4.

At Plus Two High School, she flourished as Class Monitor and achieved 11 aggregates at UCE. She then joined Light Secondary School for A-Level, specializing in Literature, Entrepreneurship and Art, earning 13 points at UACE.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Library and Information Systems at Kyambogo University, Nuswaibah merges technological fluency with service-oriented leadership. Her journey reflects discipline, community advocacy, and an unwavering drive to uplift others.
